Recreating Your Dreams

JAIPUR

The Pink City

With its age-old palaces, magnificent forts and grand mansions, Jaipur seems absolutely perfect for the most awaited wedding celebrations. It gives you a chance to get married like kings and queens at top-notch venues that aren’t anything brief of picture-perfect backdrops.
Adding to the grandeur of the ceremonies would be the local pagdi-walas, mehndi artists, folk dancers, sarangi players and more. A plus is that your guests can reach Jaipur in a breeze by air and train and even by car.

UDAIPUR

The City of Lake

Of all destinations in India that are known to be perfect for weddings, Udaipur always comes out on top and for good reason. Its clear blue skies, glorious palaces and breathtaking lakes make the most splendid setting for a regal ceremony.

Rest assured, your wedding pictures will turn out to be super colourful and vibrant! An advantage is your guests will have a lot to do once the festivities are over, from going on sightseeing tours to indulging in exciting activities like boating on Pichola Lake.

JIM CORBET

29 Feb 2020

If you have always dreamt of a destination wedding but at the same time you don’t wish to splurge, then Jim Corbett is just the perfect place for you. Fast emerging as a popular affordable destination for weddings in India, this tree-laden land will provide you and your partner the perfect opportunity to celebrate one of the biggest days of your life away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
Imagine taking pheras under a beautiful mandap surrounded by forested hills amidst chilly mountain air!

RISHIKESH

29 Feb 2020

Rishikesh is one of the most sacred cities in India and you might find it sanctimonious to begin your marital journey from here. It would indeed be fascinating to take the pheras on the shore of Ganga River underneath the stunning sky.
The sound of the river flowing by coupled with the chanting of mantras and ringing of bells will add an element of spirituality to your wedding ceremony that will be unparalleled. There are many perfect wedding venues in Rishikesh for you to choose from.

GOA

29 Feb 2020

When talking about getting married by the sea, there’s no way you can’t think about marrying your special someone in Goa. It is such a mixed bag of pristine beaches, breathtaking sunsets, historical churches, beautiful resorts and verdant forests that you can have a variety of pictures for your wedding and pre-wedding shoot.
You can also revel in Goa’s party vibe by organising the haldi and mehndi festivities by the pool, rain dances in the middle of the sea or an endless night of cocktails on the beach.

Every love story is beautiful, but yours should be unique.
